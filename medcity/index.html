About Us

Grandeur International is one of the leading distributor of Medical Equipments, reagents and consumables products from brand leaders worldwide. Contributing significantly to the society through healthcare, we are trying our level best to create strongest marketing and sales network in the state of Qatar. As an ISO 9001:2008 certified Company, we believes in ensuring the highest quality standards of products and services to its Customers and is committed to continually improving these services to all its Customers, Principals and Employees alike. .
